My passion for technology and creativity began with a desire to learn, build, and solve problems. Through formal education and years of self-driven growth, I’ve developed a strong foundation in programming, web development, graphic design, video editing, and digital media. My time at Broward College has been more than an academic experience—it has been a transformative journey that has shaped me into a lifelong learner, problem solver, and creative professional committed to continuous growth.
